Sean's Room @ 20Mission





Features

20Mission (The House)

  • 1 Bedroom @ 20Mission, a 40-Bedroom Co-Living House
  • Vibrant, co-living community of 40 SF creatives in their 20s and 30s
  • At corner of 20th & Mission St: the center of the Mission District of SF
  • Six blocks from the sunny, large, beautiful Dolores Park
  • Public transit hub: At the limited stop of two major bus lines, and just 4 blocks to BART (SF's subway system that connects to SFO Airport and East Bay)

The Room

  • Four windows of beautiful sunlight overlooking center of Mission
  • Best room in the 40-bedroom house
  • 3 Hammocks, 2 convertible-bed sofas, 1 memory foam bed
  • Walk-in Closet turned into Standing Desk/Office & Mini-Kitchen
  • Projector/Projector Screen and Sound System
  • Common Areas: Kitchen, Rooftop Courtyard, Roof, Bathrooms, and Living Room
